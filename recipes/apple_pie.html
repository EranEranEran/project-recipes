Here's how to make an Apple pie!

You're going to have all of these below ingredients...

  • Sugar
  • Salt
  • Butter
  • Flour
  • Egg
  • 1 tbsp. water

Steps

  1. Put sugar, salt, flour, egg, water in a bowl
  2. Mix together, then knead the dough til flat
  3. Put apples over the dough
  4. Put in center of over at 425
